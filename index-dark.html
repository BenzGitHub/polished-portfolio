About Me

Ben Zamora

Ben Zamora has over 16 years of experience in logistics, inventory, shipping/receiving, and production chain management. Over the last year he has added to his invaluable work skills to include app development, computer programing, back end and front end web design from an accredited Certificate Program through the University of Denver. Ben also holds a B.A. in Mass Communications from Metro State University.

A natural team player, Ben is often a leader in his field of logistics as he understands the responsibility of an efficient, competent team player. This has served him well with his newer technology role as he continues to demonstrate his value in his ability to work in a team. A quick learner and agile problem solver, you can always rely on Ben to be a flexible and responsive worker who can maneuver fluid work projects that are faced paced and have tight deadlines.
